Splitwise

Welcome to Splitwise BetaGeeks

Splitwise is a highly interactive app which let's you keep track of your shared expenses.

Often it happens that you share your expenses with your friends and colleagues and have to keep track of every one of them.

Its quite difficult once you rack up a lot of to and fro expenses. Well that is exactly when you want to use Splitwise!

Features Sign in to our app using your Google account Add a friend to your Splitwise account Add an expense you shared with a friend from the Add Expenses page Split the expense you shared with your friend equally or unequally Stay updated on who owes you and who you owe Keep track of how much money you owe to your friends or how much your friend owes you Add expenses shared among multiple friends from a simple interactive UI Split expenses equally or unequally amoung multiple friends with ease Track expenses shared among multiple friends through simple individual friend balance cards
